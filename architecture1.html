Architecture

The palace showcases traditional Maratha architecture, with grand facades, courtyards, and intricate woodwork. It embodies the elegance and grandeur of Maratha royal residences.The architecture of DKTE reflects a blend of modern educational infrastructure and the cultural heritage of Ichalkaranji. While the buildings are constructed with a focus on functionality, to accommodate the needs of technical and textile education, there are elements that pay homage to the region's rich textile history.
