Cultural Significance

Rajwada Ichalkaranji was a center of cultural and social activities in the region. It played a pivotal role in the local administration and was a hub for cultural gatherings and celebrations.DKTE reflects the values of technical education and skill development that are central to Ichalkaranji’s growth. The institute encourages collaboration with industry, promotes entrepreneurship, and helps preserve the city’s reputation as a textile powerhouse
