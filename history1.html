History

Rajwada Ichalkaranji was built by the Ghorpade dynasty, who ruled Ichalkaranji. The Ghorpades were prominent Maratha nobles, and the palace served as their royal residence and administrative center.

The palace reflects the rich history of the Maratha empire and the Ghorpade family's contribution to the region's cultural and political landscape.
